- Most failures are output-contract failures: generated prompts often omit exact `outputs/result.csv`, `result.rds`, or long-template `summary.csv` requirements, and generated code writes plausible alternate filenames.
- Internal or non-exported helper tasks are fragile: `aNCA:::` and `get(..., asNamespace("admiral"))` requirements are missing or contradicted in several current prompts.
- Simple prompts frequently preserve the filename contract but lack the computational contract, so they produce generic input echoes or mapping tables instead of reference-shaped outputs.
- Several embedded fixtures are semantically unhelpful placeholders (`AVAL`, `USUBJID`, generic `x` columns), which amplifies model guessing when the prompt is not explicit.
